BOSTON RED SOX AT BALTIMORE ORIOLES — 3-4

Red Sox Fall Short, 4-3, at Baltimore

The Baltimore Orioles topped the Boston Red Sox, 4-3, in American League action at Oriole Park at Camden Yards. The game was highlighted by the performance of Baltimore pitcher Jorge Vindiola. Vindiola, now 8-10, was the winner. In 6.1 innings, he gave up 3 hits, while striking out 8, walking 4, and allowing 1 run. Picking up the save was 25-year old right-handed pitcher Juan Vergara.

Baltimore right fielder Josh Walker contributed a timely at-bat in the bottom of the sixth. With two down, he hit a fastball to center field for a run-scoring single. It was his 108th hit of the season and put the Orioles ahead, 4-0.

"I was just taking it batter by batter, just enjoying the moment," said Vindiola.

The third-place Orioles now have a win-loss record of 52-62.
