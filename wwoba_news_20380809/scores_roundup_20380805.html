CHICAGO WHITE SOX AT LOS ANGELES ANGELS — 3-4

Angels Get Dramatic 4-3 Triumph

The Los Angeles Angels used the hitting of designated hitter Robby Conn to subdue the Chicago White Sox, 4-3, in 10 innings. For the game, Conn was 5 for 6 with 5 singles, while adding 2 RBI and scoring once. Los Angeles is now 53-57.
