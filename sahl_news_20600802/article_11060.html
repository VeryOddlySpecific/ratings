Trujillo Honored by American League

StatsPlus

Trujillo Honored by American League Sunday, August 1st , 2060 A newcomer to the league, Andy Trujillo sewed up the American League Rookie of the Month Award today after posting stout numbers last month as a starter for Baltimore.

In July he had a fine 2-1 mark as he compiled a 3.00 ERA. Trujillo also tossed 13 strikeouts in 12 innings.

This year, Andy Trujillo has been credited with 28 saves. In 44 relief appearances and 41.2 innings pitched, he has a 3-6 won-lost mark, 5.18 ERA and 54 strikeouts
