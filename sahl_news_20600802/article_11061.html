NL Chooses Rookie of the Month

StatsPlus

NL Chooses Rookie of the Month Sunday, August 1st , 2060 New York starter David Care really threw the ball well in July, earning himself the nod as National League Rookie of the Month.

The 28-year-old had 2 wins and no losses in 29 innings, put up 29 strikeouts, and issued 9 walks. He also crafted a fine 0.62 ERA and held opposing hitters to a .137 batting average.

In 12 starts this year, Care has recorded a 5-4 record and a 2.92 ERA.
