Star of the Week Awarded in SOS
Monday, April 28th , 2036
Vandalia fans have something to crow about today as Vandalia designated hitter Alex Trujillo nabbed the Stats Only South Player of the Week Award for his performance over the last seven days.

"I know there are times when it's hard to lay off pitches. Everyone goes through stretches when he's too eager to put the ball in play. You know, though, I feel like this past week I was able to get in the right frame of mind and just let things come naturally," said Trujillo.

He posted a .444 average (12-for-27) this past week with 4 home runs, 7 RBIs, and 7 runs scored.

This year, Trujillo is batting .347 with 10 home runs.
