Maldonado Thrilled, Remains with Orioles
Monday, November 26th , 2040
Baltimore fans celebrated as word of a signing became reality. Edgar Maldonado won't be pulling up stakes anytime soon, as he has signed a 3-year extension at a pay rate of $28,533,333 per annum.

Since he is widely seen as an integral part of the club, Baltimore officials had to keep Maldonado from jumping ship.

In 1501 career games Maldonado is hitting at a .283 pace with 1571 hits with 319 doubles, 28 triples and 229 homers while scoring 978 runs and racking up 776 RBIs. He logged 843 walks and owns a .383 on-base percentage.

Serna Finalizes Extension with Astros
Monday, November 26th , 2040
Fletcher Serna inked a deal that will keep the seasoned veteran on the Astros roster another 2 years. Earlier today team personnel announced that the 34-year-old had settled on a $10,500,000-a-year deal to play at Minute Maid Park.

It's no secret Serna is getting older, but he could still contribute a lot to the franchise.

During his career, Serna has been credited with 311 saves, while compiling a 59-64 won-lost mark.
