NORFOLK ATLANTICS AT PHILADELPHIA HORNETS — 7-3

Norfolk Beats Philadelphia, 7-3

The Norfolk Atlantics beat the Philadelphia Hornets, 7-3, at The Hornets Nest. The win was due in part to left fielder Steve MacIntosh, who was 2-3 with a double, a walk, and was hit by a pitch. He drove in 2 runs and scored once. The victory gives the Atlantics a record of 47-45.

Norfolk took a 3-1 lead on MacIntosh's 15th double of the season. The 2-run double, which he hit off Mike Herbert in the top of the sixth, propelled Norfolk to the win.

"I just tried to see the ball and put a good swing on it," said MacIntosh.
