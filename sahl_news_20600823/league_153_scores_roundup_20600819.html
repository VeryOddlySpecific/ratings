BOSTON RED SOX AT TEXAS RANGERS — 6-4

Behind the hitting of Chris Haarala, the Boston Red Sox stopped the Texas Rangers, 6-4. Haarala was 2 for 2 with 2 singles and 2 walks. He scored 2 times for the Red Sox. Had Haarala not come through in the top of the eighth, the outcome might have changed. Instead, with two down, Haarala hit a run-scoring single. The Red Sox never looked back after taking the 5-4 lead.

"I was just going up there trying to see good pitches, that was about it," said Haarala.
