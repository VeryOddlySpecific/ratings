Ware Celebrates Grand Opening of Restaurant
Tuesday, January 22nd , 2041
Will Ware says it has always been his dream to open his own restaurant. Today, that dream became a reality as he opened the doors of his new eatery, Ware's Restaurant and Grill.

"I wanted to combine my love of food with my love of baseball to create a fun, yet classy, dining experience that the whole family can enjoy," explained the proud new owner.

Ware's Restaurant and Grill's menu features both ballpark-inspired cuisine and traditional favorites. The walls are adorned with Sea Dogs memorabilia and other mementos from Ware's career.

"If you're into baseball nostalgia or just love great food in a friendly atmosphere, this is going to be the place for you," said Ware.

Plus, you might just bump into the Sea Dogs starting pitcher himself.

"Oh yeah, I'll be in here a lot. Whether it's baseball or business, I'm definitely a hands-on kind of guy."

Santos Agrees to 4-Year Pact with Philadelphia Brotherhood
Monday, January 21st , 2041
Philadelphia announced the signing of catcher Jorge Santos earlier today, ending speculation on who they would acquire to fill the void at that position.

Santos will earn $8,250,000 per year, and will be with the club for 4 years.

Santos has compiled a career batting average of .224 with 552 hits, 48 homers and 224 RBIs.

RF Bohling Signs with Stoppers
Monday, January 21st , 2041
Team insiders were tight-lipped prior to today's deal that brought 31-year-old right fielder Anthony Bohling to Illinois.
