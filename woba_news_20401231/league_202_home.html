Minnesota Signs Kim for 4 Years
Tuesday, December 25th , 2040
The Minnesota Twins have finished a deal with All Star-caliber starting pitcher Sung-ho Kim that will extend his stay in Minnesota at least 4 years and will pay out $204,000,000.

Kim has posted 119 victories and 66 defeats in his career with a 2.84 ERA. He also has fanned 2650 batters in 1737.1 innings and given up 1186 hits.
