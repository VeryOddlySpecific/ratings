NEWARK BEARS AT SYRACUSE CHIEFS — 9-13

Getting 5 hits in a game has always been memorable feat in baseball. In the Syracuse's 13-9 win over Newark, right fielder Jordan Lauck was red-hot, scorching them for hit after hit at Victory Field.

"To hit the ball consistently well, you have to relax," Lauck said afterward. "I was relaxed for the first four hits, but I have to admit, I got a little tight when I realized I had a chance for five. On the last at-bat, I was ready to swing at anything close."

Jordan Lauck singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, struck out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Anrique Gonzalez in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.

This year, Lauck has hit at a .276 pace and produced 50 RBIs, 16 home runs and scored 46 runs.
