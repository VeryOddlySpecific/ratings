Mort Wilson Lost for Season

Mort Wilson Lost for Season Sunday, August 23rd , 2071 "Sore." That's how Mort Wilson described his situation to BNN after he got a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament throwing a pitch against the Triplets. Team officials say they expect Wilson to be out of commission for about 11 months.

For the season, Wilson has logged 2 wins and 2 losses and struck out 29 batters in 29.2 innings while compiling a 4.55 ERA.
