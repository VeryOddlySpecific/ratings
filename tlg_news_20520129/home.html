Done Deal: Leos for Barnes
Monday, January 29th , 2052
34-year old RHP Xavier Leos will now be playing for the Minnesota Twins and 20-year old minor league RHP Shawn Barnes will be suiting up for the Pittsburgh Pirates. The trade was announced today at a press conference by club officials.


Leos's career stats shape up like this: In 695 innings, he has struck out 811, walked 351 and saved 151 games, while compiling a 60-54 record and a 3.16 ERA.

Nationals Pay $10,200,000 Per Season for 2B Frare
Wednesday, January 24th , 2052
Washington announced the signing of second baseman Dylan Frare earlier today, ending speculation on who they would acquire to fill the void at that position.

Frare will earn $10,200,000 per year, and will be with the club for 7 years.

A check of his lifetime stats shows Frare has played in 958 games with a .239 batting average. Among his 867 hits are 271 home runs. He has also scored 554 runs and driven in 686 runs.
